# ADHD and Frontend Development

In this post, we'll explore how neurodiversity affects software development.

## Strengths

1. **Hyperfocus**: When we're interested in a problem, we can immerse ourselves in it for hours on end.
2. **Creativity**: It's often easier to come up with out-of-the-box solutions.

## Challenges

1. **Time Blindness**: It can sometimes be difficult to estimate how long a task will take.
2. **Context Switching**: Switching from one task to another can drain a significant amount of energy.
